Some Frequently Asked Questions
Why can't I log into my account?
Please make sure you are using the correct ELPIS account and password when signing in.

If you receive a message that says your email address/password isn't recognised, there are a few things worth checking:
  • Make sure you are using the same email address and password you registered with.
  • If you can't remember your password, click on the 'forgotten password' link on the sign in page. You'll be prompted to enter your email address and we'll send you an email with a link that will allow you to create a new password for your account.
  • If you enter your email address and/or password incorrectly three times, your account will be locked for 30 minutes.
Browser Support
At ELPIS, we're committed to being ahead of the game. Our teams make use of the newest technologies to improve your experience. Modern, up-to-date browsers allow you to take full advantage of this as well as providing improved security and performance.

Using a deprecated browser will result in a sub-optimal experience where some features may not work as intended.

How can I make changes to the details on my account?
You can change the details on your account at any time in My Account. To make changes to your password, email address, delivery address or payment method, follow these simple steps:
1. Log into your account.
2. Once you're logged into your account, you can make as many changes to your account information as you'd like.
Can I contact you if I'm not an ELPIS account holder?
We're happy to hear from anyone who has a general query about shopping with ELPIS, however if you are contacting us about an order or an account then you do need to be the ELPIS account holder. We also ask that you use the email address that you registered with us so we can verify your details.
If our FAQ's did not answer your question or you have any feedback. Please contact our friendly customer care team via email or phone.

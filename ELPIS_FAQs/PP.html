PAYMENT & COUPONS Frequently Asked Questions
What are the modes of payment available for online purchase?
To order online, we have two (2) payment methods available:
1. Online payment via credit/debit card:
  For credit cards, we accept Visa, Mastercard, JCB and AMEX.
  For debit cards, Visa and Mastercard only.

2. Cash on Delivery (COD)
Can I pay using several cards/split payment between cash and card?
Option for installment, split payment, or using multiple credit cards is not available. We are continuously improving our service and we are looking to expand our modes of payment in the future.

We accept major credit cards and debit cards in settling your purchases.
Why was my payment declined?
It might be the below reasons:

1. Card info are incorrect, kindly check expiration date, billing address, and security code
 (Security code on the back of your Visa/Master card).
2. Card issuer bank declined your payment.
3. If possible, we advise you to try paying with PayPal or Online Bank Transfer.
Why do I have to verify my payment on my order?
Hello, we apologize for any inconvenience. It is required by your bank, you can kindly have a check with them. Also, please check your email and reply with the requested documents.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
How to use coupons?
At the checkout page, it has a coupon code and points box on the right side of the page. Please type in the correct coupon code or points amount, then click "apply".

Note: Every 100 points = PHP150. You can pay for up to 70% of your purchase with bonus points at checkout.
How do I earn coupons?
Birthday Coupon
Register your birthday in the site, and you will receive your PHP 300 Birthday coupon on the first day of your birth month. (For example, if your birthday is on April 25, 1990, you will receive your birthday coupon on April 1, 2022). Please be sure to register prior to your birthday month to claim the coupon. Otherwise you will receive it on the following year. To claim the coupon, a minimum spend of PHP 3,000 is required. Coupon validity is 1-2 months from acquisition date.

Welcome 300 Coupon
If you download the UNIQLO APP & create a UNIQLO Account for the first time, you will receive a Php 300 off welcome coupon, redeemable with a minimum spend of Php 3,000.00. For repeat downloads, you will be able to enjoy member benefits other than the welcome coupon. This coupon can be redeemed within 1-2 months. If you cannot see any coupons, it is possible that you did not provide your app consent to receive coupons from us.

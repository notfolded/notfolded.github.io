DELIVERY Frequently Asked Questions
Where is my order?
We will email you tracking info after the order being shipped out. Also, you can track it via "My Orders". If there have no updates on page tracking after your order is shipped out. The reason is due to your order was sent from our overseas warehouses, you'll be able to see more tracking updates once it arrives in the Philippines. It's under the normal shipping process, no worries. Please give some time for the courier to update the latest tracking info after reaching the next station, hope you will receive your package soon and love the wonderful item.
How long does delivery usually take?
Below are the estimated time frames from date of purchase:
 Metro Manila: 3-5 working days
 Luzon: 5-8 working days
 Visayas: 8-10 working days
 Mindanao: 10-12 working days

We can only provide estimates and more detailed information may be viewed on your order status once the items have been shipped. We will allocate additional days for cases of unforeseen events such as natural calamities.
Can I select the courier for delivery?
ELPIS has partnered with NINJA VAN as our main courier for all online deliveries. We also have AIR21 to support with deliveries. You cannot choose your preferred courier for your delivery at the moment. If you want your items to be delivered to you, kindly check Ninja Van's website for the complete coverage. If your delivery address is not covered by Ninja Van, we may attempt to deliver it via AIR21 only if you paid online or via pay-in-store. To receive the package, you must present a valid ID.
How much is the shipping fee?
We offer free shipping for a single receipt purchase of PHP 2,500 and above. For purchases below PHP 2,500, the shipping fee is PHP 120 to areas covered by our authorized couriers in the Philippines.
Can I change the delivery destination?
No, you cannot change the delivery destination or the delivery method once the order has been placed.

However, you may cancel your order within the allowable time frame, and re-order with the updated delivery destination.

To cancel your order, kindly follow these steps:
 1. Click on [MY ACCOUNT]
 2. Click on [ORDER HISTORY]
 3. The Cancel button will appear if your order is eligible for cancellation.

Once order cancellation has been confirmed, we will send you an email to confirm the cancellation and process your payment refund.
Can I have my items delivered in another country?
We do not ship outside of the Philippines. We only offer delivery services within the areas covered by our authorized couriers (Ninja Van and Air21) in the Philippines.
Can someone other than myself receive the delivery of my purchased items?
Yes. The person appointed or authorized to receive the delivery package at your nominated address just need to present the order confirmation email or the Order History.
If our FAQ's did not answer your question or you have any feedback. Please contact our friendly customer care team via email or phone.
